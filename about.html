About Us

Our Vision

Welcome to Harihara Yoga, which arms you with timeless yoga techniques in a swanky, state-of-the-art studio ambience. At Harihara, it will be our mission to make everyone feel lighter, fresher and stronger by offering group and personalized yoga classes, every week.

Yoga For All

Harihara Yoga is perhaps the only place in Townsville, where you have the flexibility of learning classical yoga from a variety of schools including Iyengar, Sivananda, Satyananda Yoga and Mysore Ashtanga. Our highly qualified yoga teachers also have the capability of customizing classes according to your health needs.
