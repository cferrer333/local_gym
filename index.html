Our Classes

Traditional Hatha Yoga

Indian traditional style well-rounded practice incorporating mantra, postures, and breathing practices that build strength, flexibility and mental concentration while allowing for plenty of relaxation.

Beginners Ashtanga

For those that are new to Ashtanga. Yoga based on eight principles and consisting of a series of poses executed in swift succession, combined with deep, controlled breathing


Resorative Yoga

A restful practice that holds yoga poses(asanas) for a longer duration using props like yoga blocks, blankets, and bolsters.



Yin Yoga

Yin Yoga is a slow-paced meditative practice. Mindful breathing induces meditative movement and introspection. Connective tissues surrounding muscle receive freshness and lightness.
